About Me

I’m Cristian, a Cloud & Kubernetes Engineer based in Barcelona, Spain. I specialize in building, automating, and securing cloud-native infrastructure, with certifications in Kubernetes (CKA/CKAD), Terraform, and Google Cloud.

With 2+ years of experience in cloud environments, I’ve supported complex GCP escalations and developed hands-on expertise through projects in AWS and GCP. My background includes validating technical solutions, managing incident escalations with strict SLAs/SLOs, and coordinating across engineering and product teams.

Beyond professional experience, I’ve designed and deployed highly available Kubernetes clusters and automated infrastructure in my personal labs, focusing on IaC with Terraform, Kubernetes administration, and multi-cloud resilience.

I’m passionate about reliability, automation, and enabling teams to deliver scalable, cost-efficient services. Currently targeting roles in Kubernetes Administration, Cloud Engineering, Platform Engineering, Site Reliability Engineering, and DevOps.
